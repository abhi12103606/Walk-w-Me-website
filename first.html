WELCOME TO THE WALK W/ME:

Welcome to THE WALK W/ME , your first stop for walking information and support. Whether you are just getting started, looking to enhance your walking routine we are here to help. Cruise around the web site.

BEGINNING A FITNESS WALKING PROGRAM

Are you ready to start walking and don't know where to begin? We'll lead the way. Walking is one of the easiest, most enjoyable, and most profitable forms of exercise. All you need is a good pair of shoes, comfortable clothing, and desire.Click here to get started.

MARATHON WALKING

Have you ever thought about walking a marathon? 70% of walkers in our survey stated a desire to train for and participate in a marathon or distance walking event. 18% of those polled previously walked in a marathon, and most of those said they would like to do it again some day. We have a variety of training schedules and additional information to get you ready for a great marathon experience.
